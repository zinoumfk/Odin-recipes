Apple Salad


Description:

Made with various types of fruit,

served either in their own juices or a syrup.

Also known as a fruit cocktail.


Ingredients:

  • 2 Red Delicious apples, cored and diced
  • 2 Granny Smith apples, cored and diced
  • 4 stalks celery, diced
  • ½ cup raisins
  • 1 cup toasted walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons walnut oil
  • 1 tablespoon cider vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon pepper

steps:

  1. Toss together the Red Delicious apples,

    Granny Smith apples, celery, raisins, and walnuts in a large bowl;

    set aside. In a small bowl, whisk together the walnut oil, cider vinegar, salt, and pepper.

    Toss the apples with the dressing to serve.
